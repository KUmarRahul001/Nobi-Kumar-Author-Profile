# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Problem statement
Readers discover Nobi Kumar through fragmented surfaces: social posts, marketplace listings, and occasional book mentions. There is no single premium destination that explains the catalog, the universe, the author story, and the reading journey.

## 2. Product goals
- Establish a credible canonical author website
- Increase book discovery and purchase intent
- Reduce friction from discovery to sample to purchase
- Provide a visually distinctive brand experience
- Support ongoing reader retention via blog and newsletter

## 3. Target users
- New readers
- Existing fans
- Reviewers / bloggers / media
- Retail buyers and booksellers
- Potential collaboration partners

## 4. Priority scope
### P0
- Home
- Books list
- Book detail pages
- Sample chapter reader
- About
- Contact
- Newsletter signup
- Search
- Dark mode
- SEO foundations

### P1
- Interactive NNU / Verma Saga map
- Blog
- Blog comments with moderation
- FAQ
- Testimonials / reviews
- Press / media kit

### P2
- Advanced filtering by series, theme, status
- Reading order visualization
- Optional tip jar / donations
- Author events page
- Reader request forms

## 5. Product principles
- Every page should have one primary action.
- Purchase links should be prominent but not intrusive.
- The universe map must be understandable in under one minute.
- Sample reading should be friction-light and mobile-friendly.
- Dark mode is not decorative; it is part of the brand.

## 6. Success metrics
- Book detail CTR from home page
- Sample chapter open rate
- Newsletter signup rate
- Search usage
- Blog return visits
- Contact form completion rate

## 7. Key differentiators
- Genre-first identity
- Cinematic design language
- Structured book universe navigation
- Strong content hierarchy and conversion path

## 8. Feature summary
### Home
Value proposition, featured book, latest release, universe teaser, CTA stack.

### Books
List, series grouping, metadata, retailer links, sample links, reading order.

### Universe Map
Interactive visual of books, series, story links, chronology.

### Blog
Articles, behind-the-scenes posts, commentary, updates, moderated comments.

### About
Author story, genre positioning, writing philosophy, official photo.

### Contact
Reader inquiry form, collaboration inquiries, media contact routing.

### Newsletter
Capture email, consent, confirmation, unsubscribe.

### Search
Search by title, series, tag, character, or theme.

## 9. Referenced business requirements
- BR-01 canonical author presence
- BR-02 drive book sales
- BR-03 support reader engagement
- BR-04 strengthen brand recall
- BR-05 create a premium thriller identity
