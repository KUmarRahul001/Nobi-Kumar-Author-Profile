# Assumptions & Constraints

## Assumptions
- The website is for a single author brand
- Purchases happen on third-party retail sites
- Most content is editorial rather than transactional
- The catalog will grow over time
- Dark mode is part of the brand identity

## Constraints
- No on-site checkout in v1
- No heavy community platform
- No dependence on a complex CMS is required at launch
- Must be manageable by an AI coding agent
- Must remain easy to update after launch
