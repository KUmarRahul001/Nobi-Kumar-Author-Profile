# Brand Guidelines

## Brand essence
Premium psychological thriller with cinematic restraint.

## Brand attributes
- Mysterious
- Intelligent
- Dark
- Premium
- Minimal
- Immersive

## Voice rules
### Do
- Use clear, evocative language
- Keep statements direct
- Maintain genre confidence
- Use short punchy lines for CTAs

### Do not
- Sound generic or corporate
- Overuse slang
- Use bright lifestyle language
- Use overly decorative wording

## Visual cues
- Charcoal black
- Deep grey
- Midnight blue
- Muted neutrals
- Subtle crimson
- Neon violet accents
- Film grain
- Low-key lighting
- Negative space

## Logo usage
- Prefer wordmark-first identity
- Keep logo readable at small sizes
- Use monochrome and inverse variants

## Photography direction
- Cinematic portraits
- Rain, fog, mirrors, corridors, libraries
- Shadow and reflection motifs
- Avoid cheerful stock-style author photos

## Typography direction
- Headlines: elegant serif
- Body: modern sans-serif
- Captions: clean, highly readable sans-serif

## Consistency rule
The same visual language should apply across website, Instagram, Facebook, book mockups, and future launch materials.
