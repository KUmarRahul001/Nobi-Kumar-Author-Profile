# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Business background
Nobi Kumar's catalog and brand are valuable, but the audience currently encounters the work across disconnected locations. A website should consolidate this into a dependable, owned channel.

## Business objectives
- Improve brand ownership
- Increase outbound purchase traffic
- Capture first-party audience data
- Build a platform for future launches
- Support media and collaboration inquiries

## Stakeholders
- Author / brand owner: Nobi Kumar
- Engineering / AI coding agent
- Future readers
- Editors / marketers
- Platform administrator

## Business rules
- The website is the canonical source of truth.
- Amazon and retailer links remain outbound; no checkout in v1.
- Blog comments require moderation.
- Newsletter requires explicit consent.
- The tone must remain thriller-focused.

## In-scope business outcomes
- Readers can buy, sample, and understand the books quickly
- The author can publish updates without code changes
- The site can scale to future series and media assets

## Out-of-scope business outcomes
- E-commerce fulfillment
- Community marketplace
- Paid memberships
- Multi-author publishing portal

## KPI targets for v1
- Visitor-to-book-click rate
- Visitor-to-sample rate
- Newsletter opt-in rate
- Blog engagement rate
- Contact form completion rate

## Operational assumptions
- Content is authored in markdown/MDX or CMS
- Forms are protected against spam
- Retail purchases are handled by third-party stores
- Search indexing is enabled for public pages
