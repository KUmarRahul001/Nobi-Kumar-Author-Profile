# Acceptance Criteria

## Homepage
- Visitor can identify Nobi Kumar and the thriller brand immediately
- Featured book is visible above the fold
- Primary actions are obvious

## Books
- Every published title is listed
- Each book has synopsis and outbound purchase links
- Sample chapter is reachable

## Universe map
- Reader can understand series connections
- Node details are readable
- Reading order is visible

## Blog
- Posts render with metadata
- Comments are moderated
- Newsletter CTA is visible

## About and contact
- Bio is clear and credible
- Contact form submits successfully
- Confirmation appears after submit

## Theme and search
- Theme preference persists
- Search returns meaningful results
