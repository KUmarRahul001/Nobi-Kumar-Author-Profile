# Executive Summary

## Project intent
Create a single, premium author website for Nobi Kumar that becomes the authoritative home for the brand, books, universe, and reader conversion. The site should feel like a cinematic psychological thriller, not a creator profile or social feed.

## Business objective
Turn scattered discovery points into one cohesive destination that:
1. introduces Nobi Kumar as an author brand,
2. showcases the catalog and sample chapters,
3. drives readers to purchase books,
4. supports ongoing reader engagement through blog and newsletter,
5. makes the Verma Saga / NNU universe easy to explore.

## Core features
- Home page with hero, latest release, featured books, and value proposition
- Books / novel list with filters and retailer links
- Book detail pages with synopsis, sample, buy links, metadata, and related titles
- Interactive novel universe map
- Blog with comments and moderation
- About page
- Contact page
- Newsletter signup
- Search
- Dark mode
- Optional social proof sections: testimonials, press, FAQs, awards, reading order

## Success definition
The project succeeds when a reader can land on the site, understand who Nobi Kumar is, discover the catalog, read a sample, navigate the universe, and click through to buy or subscribe within one session.

## Public footprint context
Current public references appear spread across marketplace and social profiles rather than a single canonical author home, so the website should consolidate biography, catalog, and discovery into one strong source of truth.
