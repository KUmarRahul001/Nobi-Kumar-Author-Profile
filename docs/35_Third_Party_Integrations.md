# Third-Party Integrations

## Required
- Email delivery provider for newsletter
- Analytics platform
- Retailer outbound links (Amazon and others)
- Spam protection service or equivalent logic
- Image CDN or optimized hosting

## Optional
- Headless CMS
- Search provider
- Social embed support
- Media kit delivery service

## Integration rules
- No third-party dependency may block the public homepage
- External services must fail gracefully
- Integrations should be swappable without major rewrite
