# Functional Requirements (FRD)

## FR-01 Home page composition
The homepage shall display an author hero, value proposition, featured book, latest release, and primary CTAs.

## FR-02 Book catalog
The site shall list all novels and allow browsing by title, series, and status.

## FR-03 Book detail pages
Each book shall have synopsis, cover, sample excerpt, metadata, reading order, and outbound buy links.

## FR-04 Sample chapter reader
The site shall provide a readable sample chapter view with smooth scrolling and progress awareness.

## FR-05 Universe map
The site shall provide an interactive story-world map connecting novels, series, and key entities.

## FR-06 Blog
The site shall publish blog posts in chronological order with categories and tags.

## FR-07 Comments
Logged or verified users may submit comments, subject to moderation.

## FR-08 Newsletter signup
Visitors may submit email addresses and consent to receive updates.

## FR-09 Contact
Visitors may submit message type, email, subject, and message body.

## FR-10 Search
Users shall search across books, posts, tags, and universe entities.

## FR-11 Dark mode
Users shall toggle and persist a dark/light preference, with dark as the default brand mode.

## FR-12 SEO metadata
Each page shall have unique title, description, canonical URL, and structured metadata.

## FR-13 Social sharing
Book and post pages shall support share-ready metadata and preview images.

## FR-14 Admin content safety
Admin actions for comment approval, post publishing, and contact triage shall be restricted.

## FR-15 Reader trust
The site shall show author bio, official links, and content legitimacy signals.

## Edge cases
- Missing book cover
- Retailer link unavailable
- Empty comments state
- Invalid subscription email
- Search with no matching results
- Universe node with no link target yet
- Very long book titles on mobile
