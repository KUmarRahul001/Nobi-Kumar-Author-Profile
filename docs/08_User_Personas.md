# User Personas

## Persona 1: New Thriller Reader
- Goal: decide whether to read Nobi Kumar
- Needs: clear genre fit, strong synopsis, sample chapter, buy button
- Pain points: time scarcity, too much text, weak first impression

## Persona 2: Returning Fan
- Goal: understand the full NNU / Verma Saga reading order
- Needs: universe map, book list, related titles, new blog updates
- Pain points: losing track of chronology and connections

## Persona 3: Reviewer / Blogger / Media
- Goal: gather accurate author and book information
- Needs: bio, press-ready summary, book descriptions, contact route
- Pain points: missing facts, scattered links, inconsistent branding

## Persona 4: Collaboration Lead
- Goal: evaluate author credibility and outreach potential
- Needs: brand professionalism, contact clarity, portfolio depth
- Pain points: amateur presentation, unclear response path

## Persona 5: Existing Newsletter Subscriber
- Goal: stay informed about new releases and updates
- Needs: email updates, launch notices, blog highlights
- Pain points: spam, too many messages, unclear subscription controls
