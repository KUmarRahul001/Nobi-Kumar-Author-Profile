# Vision Document

## Vision statement
Build a cinematic, premium author platform that makes Nobi Kumar instantly recognizable as a psychological-thriller storyteller and turns the website into the central place readers, retailers, and media use to understand the brand.

## Brand promise
Every page should feel like entering a suspense film:
- dark, elegant, and focused
- intelligent rather than noisy
- emotionally tense but polished
- immersive enough to support deep reading interest

## Strategic pillars
### 1. Canonical author identity
A clear bio, photo, genre statement, and story-world positioning.

### 2. Conversion to reading
Direct outbound purchase links, sample chapters, and prominent calls to action.

### 3. Universe depth
An interactive novel map for the NNU / Verma Saga so readers understand chronology, connections, and reading order.

### 4. Continuous engagement
Blog posts, newsletter, and updates that keep readers returning.

### 5. Discoverability
Strong SEO, structured data, and content hierarchy so the site ranks for author, book, and universe searches.

## Out of scope for v1
- In-site payments and cart checkout
- Complex membership tiers
- User-generated image uploads
- Full forum/community features
- Mobile app companion

## Desired reader outcome
A first-time visitor should leave with:
- a clear idea of what Nobi Kumar writes,
- at least one book or sample chapter opened,
- a sense that the universe is layered and intentional,
- a reason to return or subscribe.
